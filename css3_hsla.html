CSS3 HSL and HSLA

HSL is a new coloring feature in CSS3 which stands for Hue (which can go from 0 to 360) Saturation (uses a percentage) and Lightness (again uses a percentage to determine how light and how dark the color is) there is also HSLA with the A being Alpha and works the same way as RGBA.
